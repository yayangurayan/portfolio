More of About Me

I’ve always had a strong sense of determination since I was a child—whenever I set my mind on something, I wouldn’t stop until I made it happen. That mindset has shaped who I am today: a self-driven learner, an ambitious student, and someone who turns curiosity into real-world projects. I grew up believing that knowledge and consistency can open doors, which led me to explore both the financial world and creative technology as early as high school.

Now, as a Digital Business student at Universitas Negeri Medan, I pursue two passions: capital market analysis and indie game development. I actively lead investor education initiatives on campus while also creating 2D platformer games using Unity and C#. For me, business and creativity go hand in hand—I enjoy analyzing data as much as I enjoy designing levels and coding mechanics. With every project, I challenge myself to grow, stay focused, and never forget why I started.
